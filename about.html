About Tang Tutoring

Supportive, low‑stress, and actually fun — that’s how we approach learning.

We believe learning should be supportive, low-stress, and even fun. Together we combine classroom teaching, tutoring experience, and industry expertise to guide students through math, science, programming, and other subjects in a way that builds both skills and confidence.

Our tutoring approach

  • Meet students where they are. We start with a quick check‑in to understand goals and challenges.
  • Make learning click. Clear explanations, worked examples, and guided practice.
  • Build confident habits. Consistent support, study skills, and simple routines.
  • Keep parents in the loop. Brief updates and progress notes as requested.
